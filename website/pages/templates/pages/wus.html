WAKE UP SCREAMING

Wake Up Screaming or WUS is Cambridge RockSoc's own club night, which runs every third Friday of the month at the Q Club, both in an out of term. It's a great chance to meet up with members of RockSoc and the wider rock and metal community in Cambridge.
Entry is £3 for members and £4 for non-members.

DJing at WUS

Fancy trying your hand at DJing? Contact the WUS officer. If you have no experience we will put you on first set, and if we like you we will ask you back. You can also join the DJ mailing list by sending an e-mail to dj-subscribe (at) rocksoc org uk. For more information about DJing, see our DJ information page
