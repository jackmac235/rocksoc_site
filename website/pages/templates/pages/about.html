ABOUT

What is Cambridge RockSoc?

RockSoc is Cambridge's rock, metal, and alternative society!

Our aim is to bring together people interested in rock, metal and alternative music, and to support the alternative scene in Cambridge. To this end we run a monthly club night: Wake Up Screaming, organise at least one live gig per term, and have weekly pub meets to socialise. We may also organise other events such as trips to gigs elsewhere, and we attempt to keep a comprehensive record of all rock, metal, and alternative events happening in Cambridge.

What music do we cover?

Our music tastes evolve with our members, but we try to cater for alternative music not covered elsewhere in Cambridge. The spectrum of interests in RockSoc ranges from Grunge to Alternative to Classic Metal to 70s Rock to Death and Black Metal to Hardcore to Punk to Grindcore to Goth to Industrial and takes in a good few others along the way, so the chances are that whatever you like there'll be someone in RockSoc who agrees with you!

Who can join?

Anyone can join and take advantage of what we offer. We are officially a Cambridge University society, but this has no bearing on our members; if it did we would go independent!

How can I join?

Joining is cheap and easy! Membership is £5 for life (quite literally) and gets you the following benefits:

  • cheaper entry to Wake Up Screaming (£3 instead of £4)
  • cheaper entry to all of our gigs (typically £1 off)
  • a vote at the RockSoc AGM
  • reduced prices at other rock-related events in Cambridge where we can arrange it
  • your very own RockSoc membership card, making you the envy of all your friends!

To join, turn up to one of our events and speak to a member of the committee who will be able to assist.
