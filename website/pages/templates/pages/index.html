WELCOME TO CAMBRIDGE ROCKSOC!

This is the official webpage of RockSoc, the Cambridge University society for all rock, metal, punk and alternative fans. We organise termly events such as meet-ups, pub crawls, gigs and club nights. RockSoc is a great way of meeting like-minded rock and metal people in Cambridge!

Please check out RockSoc on Facebook for the most up-to-date information.

WAKE UP SCREAMING

This is our club night at the Q Club, taking place every third Friday of the month. If you are interested in DJing, contact the WUS officer. No experience is necessary for the first slot!

PUB MEETS

We meet weekly on a Thursday at a random pub in Cambridge, hailed as “RockSoc on tour”. To find out where our next pub meet is, keep an eye on the events page.

GIGS

Gigs are organised at least once a term. If you are a band looking for a slot then contact the events officer.
